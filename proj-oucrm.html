Orion's University CRM

A Tailor-Made Family Management System

Introduction
While working at Speedbridge, I got the opportunity to work on a project was for Orion's University. Their sales team lacked a standardized organizational system to manage their leads information. Existing CRM software is often too bulky, and fit the needs of an education focused social enterprise.

About Orion's University
As a social enterprise, Orion's University focuses on student learning through enriching K-12 after school programs and tutoring. They offer a wide range of programs, from physical activity to culinary classes, introducing students to subjects that might not be provided at school.

My Role
I designed the web app that would function as the customer relationships management system for OU's sales team. Using the user cases they complied, I created wireframes and designed mockups. I also got to work closely with the development team on how the system and interactions would look and function.

Family First
An important aspect to tackle while designing was the complicated nature of the students' family structures. I designed the overview to be organized by families instead of individuals. It is built to have infinite scrolling, but for more specific searching, the search bar and filtering are fixed at the top.
